About me

Hi, I'm Vishva, an actor from Tamil Nadu, India. My journey in acting began in 2017 when I started as a junior artist in the film Velaikkaran. This experience ignited my passion for the craft, leading me to study acting through books and theater. In 2022, I signed on as a character artist for the Tamil film Rangoli, which was released in 2023. I'm excited to continue growing in this field and share my stories with you.
